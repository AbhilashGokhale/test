Research and development is an on-going process at Atrimed Biotech. The research is focused on finding new therapeutic molecules and improving upon the existing ones for greater effectiveness and approval.
Atrimed Biotech is involved in widespread research of phytochemicals and their applications in the field of human medicine, currently focusing on developing therapeutics for an autoimmune disease that leads to inflammation, such as psoriasis; and in skin infections, such as acne, and other bacterial and fungal infections. Our research is also focused on other aspects of human health, such as wound healing.


Atrimed Biotech has gained potential leads to breakthrough remedies in treating psoriasis and acne. These leads will be interpreted and analyzed by our team, in order to elucidate the mechanism of action of the therapeutics, using several chemical, biochemical, immunological and tissue culture techniques. The research team has a great insight into ancient knowledge and expertise in human diseases, plant chemistry and biotechnology. We corroborate old concepts through modern methods, which include lab tests and clinical trials in collaboration with highly reputed independent medical research centers and hospitals. We are thus able to translate old into new and fuse them.
